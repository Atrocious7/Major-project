False Criminal Accusations

  • False Rape Allegation: Legal remedy under Article 21, Section 357A CrPC.
  • False Kidnapping Case: Rights under Section 182 IPC for false complaints.
  • False Attempted Murder Case: Compensation claims and defamation suit.

False Domestic & Family Disputes

  • False Dowry Case: Defamation suit under Section 500 IPC.
  • False Domestic Violence Case: Right to contest under Domestic Violence Act.
  • False Child Abuse Allegation: Legal action under Juvenile Justice Act.

False Workplace & Defamation Cases

  • False Sexual Harassment Case: Action under Section 182 IPC.
  • False Defamation Cases: Protection under Section 499 IPC.
